PHYSICS: IMPORTANT NUMERICALS

1. Time period of a simple pendulum of length 1m (g = 10 m/s²) is:

A) 1 sec B) 2 sec C) π sec D) √2 sec

✅ Correct Answer: C) π sec

Use T = 2π√(L/g) = 2π√(1/10) ≈ 2π/√10 ≈ π sec

Trick: If L = 1 m and g = 10, T almost always comes near π seconds.

2. Frequency of an object vibrating with time period 0.2 seconds is:

A) 2 Hz B) 5 Hz C) 10 Hz D) 20 Hz

✅ Correct Answer: B) 5 Hz

Frequency f = 1/T = 1/0.2 = 5 Hz

Trick: 0.2 is 1/5, so flip it to get 5 Hz.

3. What is the wavelength of a wave having speed 340 m/s and frequency 170 Hz?

A) 0.5 m B) 1 m C) 2 m D) 3 m

✅ Correct Answer: C) 2 m

λ = v/f = 340 / 170 = 2 m

Trick: Always divide speed by frequency to get wavelength.

4. Which quantity remains constant during SHM?

A) Displacement B) Acceleration C) Frequency D) Velocity

✅ Correct Answer: C) Frequency

Only frequency remains fixed in SHM; others keep changing.

Trick: In SHM, frequency and time period are always constant.

5. The speed of sound in air at 20°C is approximately:

A) 300 m/s B) 320 m/s C) 340 m/s D) 360 m/s

✅ Correct Answer: C) 340 m/s

At 20°C, standard sound speed in air ≈ 340 m/s

Trick: Just memorize: 0°C → 331 m/s, 20°C → ~340 m/s.

6. Maximum velocity in SHM is given by:

A) ωA B) A/ω C) A²ω D) Aω²

✅ Correct Answer: A) ωA

v_max = ωA (angular frequency × amplitude)

Trick: Max velocity? Multiply amplitude by ω (vmax = ωA).

7. A tuning fork produces 4 beats with another fork of frequency 256 Hz. The frequency of the second fork is:

A) 260 Hz B) 252 Hz C) Both A and B D) 258 Hz

✅ Correct Answer: C) Both A and B

Beat frequency = |f₁ - f₂| → f₂ = 256 ± 4 = 260 or 252

Trick: Always add & subtract beat freq from given fork.

8. In resonance, the amplitude of vibration:

A) Increases B) Decreases C) Remains constant D) Becomes zero

✅ Correct Answer: A) Increases

At resonance, frequency matches natural frequency → max energy transfer → amplitude increases.

Trick: Resonance = Max amplitude = Dangerous in buildings!

9. A string vibrates in fundamental mode. If its length is 1.5 m, the wavelength is:

A) 1.5 m B) 3.0 m C) 0.75 m D) 6.0 m

✅ Correct Answer: B) 3.0 m

In fundamental mode, string = 1/2 λ → λ = 2 × L = 3 m

Trick: Always double the length for λ in fundamental mode.

10. Which of these is NOT a characteristic of wave motion?

A) Energy transfer B) Mass transfer C) Wavelength D) Frequency

✅ Correct Answer: B) Mass transfer

Waves transfer energy, not mass.

Trick: Water wave example: cork bobs up & down — doesn’t travel.

1. Find the magnitude of vector A = 3i + 4j.

A) 5 B) 7 C) 12 D) 1

✅ Correct Answer: A) 5

|A| = √(3² + 4²) = √(9 + 16) = √25 = 5

Trick: This is the famous 3-4-5 triangle.

2. If vector A = 6i + 8j, find its unit vector.

✅ Correct Answer: A) 0.6i + 0.8j

Magnitude = √(36 + 64) = √100 = 10 → Unit vector = (6/10)i + (8/10)j = 0.6i + 0.8j

Trick: Divide each component by magnitude.

3. Find the angle between vectors A = 5i and B = 5j.

A) 0° B) 45° C) 90° D) 180°

✅ Correct Answer: C) 90°

Dot product = 0 → cosθ = 0 → θ = 90°

Trick: i and j are always perpendicular.

4. If two vectors A and B have same direction and |A| = 6, |B| = 2, then |A + B| = ?

A) 3 B) 4 C) 8 D) 10

✅ Correct Answer: C) 8

Same direction → |A + B| = |A| + |B| = 6 + 2 = 8

Trick: Add directly if both in same direction.

5. Find the dot product of A = 2i + 3j and B = 4i + j.

A) 11 B) 8 C) 10 D) 13

✅ Correct Answer: D) 13

A·B = (2)(4) + (3)(1) = 8 + 3 = 11

Trick: Multiply same components and add.

6. Resultant of two equal vectors at 60° angle is 10N. Each vector is of magnitude:

A) 5 N B) 6 N C) 8 N D) 10 N

✅ Correct Answer: B) 6 N

R = √(A² + A² + 2A²cosθ) → 10 = √(2A²(1 + cos60)) = √(2A² × 1.5) = √(3A²) = A√3 → A = 10/√3 ≈ 5.77 ≈ 6 N

Trick: Use R = A√(2(1 + cosθ)) for equal vectors.

7. Two perpendicular vectors A = 4N and B = 3N. Find magnitude of resultant.

A) 5 N B) 6 N C) 7 N D) 8 N

✅ Correct Answer: A) 5 N

R = √(4² + 3²) = √(16 + 9) = √25 = 5 N

Trick: Classic Pythagorean triple — 3-4-5

✅ Correct Answer: B) 10 m/s

Trick: From rest → v = a × t

Trick: Negative sign shows deceleration.
